In this study, you will act as a demand planner. A statistical forecast system provides demand forecasts for a product across several periods.

You will first observe forecast-performance information for several blocks of periods through Period 30. During these observation blocks, you are not required to make any forecast decisions. Your first forecast decision will begin at Period 31.

Before moving through the remaining observation blocks, carefully observe the system's performance for Periods 1–5.
