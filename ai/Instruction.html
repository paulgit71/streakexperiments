In this study, you will act as a demand planner. A statistical forecast system provides demand forecasts for a product across several periods.

You will first observe forecast-performance information for several blocks of periods through Period 30. During these observation blocks, you are not required to make any forecast decisions, but You must answer the two slider questions below the table based on the SYSTEM FORECAST accuracy. Your first forecast decision will begin at Period 31.

Before moving through the remaining periods, carefully observe the system's forecast and its performance for Periods 1–5.

System APE means the absolute percentage error of the statistical system forecast relative to actual demand. The smaller the APE, the better the forecast accuracy . After each block, you will answer two slider questions about your perceived forecast-system accuracy and confidence.
